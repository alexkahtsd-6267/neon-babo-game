Mana Rules
Regen delay happens after you STOP spending mana (default 0.4s).
Attack allowed while mana > 10. Molotov + Grenade use no mana.
Ball spell uses the same per-shot mana cost as a normal shot and can be held.
Sniper uses a flat mana cost and hits anything in the beam line for fixed damage.
High-speed projectiles can also use an invisible delayed line-hitbox.
Size boost activates above the chosen speed threshold and can be toggled with U.
